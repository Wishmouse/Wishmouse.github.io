How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

Honestly it didn’t affect the site redesign that much. Time is precious at the moment which means I complete the task as quickly and simply as I can, while following the instructions and principles in the lesson. My task is to finish the assignment by Friday of each week and if there is time on the weekend I get to tweak and pretty-fy. I look at others blogs and am impressed with their achievements and inspired to give their ideas a try.

What did you think about mediation before reading chapter 2?

Not a huge fan of mediation, I have read a lot about it, have friends swear by it and have had it recommended by medical professionals. Sitting and trying to focus on not focussing for any length of time is a challenge, I have found other ways of cutting down the background chatter like running and swimming. Both activities bring a sense of calm, you zone out, breath and your mind slowly drifts off into a meditative state. The joys of being an active relaxer.

What new things have you learnt about mediation?

That it actively affects the composition of your brain and cuts down the blink speed. That’s freaky stuff.

Did any of the suggested mediation techniques stand out to you?

When distracted, take five breaths before acting on it. Also, its perfectly okay to loose focus, once you've noticed, dismiss and refocus.
