Self control

Don't feed the MONSTER

The concept is simple, when we get stressed, nervous or angry instead of feeding into those emotions and let them become all consuming. By all consuming I mean taking over our thoughts and actions and letting them control how we behave. We need to feel them for what they are, look for the body sensation that comes with them (like a racing heart beat), feel the changes in our body, feel the changes in our emotion, evauate what has caused them. Five steps are :

  • Stop
  • Breathe
  • Notice
  • Reflect
  • Respond

By the time you have done all 5, you are acting with more rationale than emotion and therefore able to handle the situation with a lot more maturity instead of going with an emotion driven reaction.
