Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?
Emotional Intelligence is about getting to know yourself and becoming self aware. If you think that sounds a bit new-agey, then the next sentence is going to blow your mind : Happiness can be trained! Every emotion that we experience has a physiological response, by the time we realise we are stressed its almost too late to back track. By being aware of our body behaviour we can manage our emotions more effectively. The more we are able to managing our emotions (and response), the higher our EI. And people with a high EI are just do darn likable.
How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?
Two minutes is not time at all, until you have to sit down and stop the internal chatter. First attempt was dismal. Attempt number 2 was a success, just slid into a breathing rhythm and felt body sensations, I think its going to be my thing.
