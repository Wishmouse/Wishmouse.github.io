Motivation: The reason I get up in the morning

Pleasure, passion, purpose

Seems I am pleasure driven, no surprises there, each year I set myself a list of New Years resolutions, which I keep (probably the only person alive who does). They are always just outside of my world and take effort to achieve. I don’t seem to flow, its all about the high and moving onto the next thing and its never a little challenge. Do I live the life of a rock star…. Well no, but I seem to burn the candle at both ends. Time to do some soul searching and find out why. I noticed that my Passions are missing, a big gaping hole, during this exercise I couldn’t think of one thing that I was totally passionate about, that’s not good. Makes me appear flighty and lacking commitment. Purpose… that brings a smile, I crave purpose, look for it at every opportunity, hunt it down at work, look for it in my relationships. This has been an interesting exercise.
